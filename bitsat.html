About BITSAT 2023 BITSAT 2023: BITS Pilani will announce the BITSAT 2023 exam date at bitsadmission.com. The application forms to apply for BITS Admission Test 2023 is expected to be released in February. However, the authorities have not yet announced the BITSAT 2023 application form release date. The authorities will release the BITSAT 2023 exam dates along with official brochure.

Students who have passed class 12th with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as compulsory subjects having 75 percent in aggregate and a minimum of 60 percent in each paper will be eligible to apply for the BITSAT 2023 exam. As per the BITSAT 2023 exam pattern, the exam will be conducted for three hours comprising 130 multiple-choice questions. Along with the exam pattern, of BITSAT candidates must also go through the BITSAT 2023 syllabus for exam preparations. Students should also attempt BITS Pilani 2023 Admission Test mock tests to analyze their preparation level.

    BITSAT 2023 Counselling Mode of Counselling: Online, Offline
    Registered candidates will be able to attend the BITSAT 2023 counselling in online mode. The counselling process of BITSAT 2023 comprises registration, filling of personal details, submission of qualifying exam marks, choice filling, payment of BITSAT counselling fee, and allotment of seats. The seat allotment of BITSAT 2023 will be released in the form of Iteration rounds. After each Iteration round, admission and waitlist will be published for the benefit of the candidate.
